AllTold
A calorie tracker that doesn't judge you.

Don't hold the user hostage

AllTold tracks what you eat. It doesn't have an opinion about it. No streaks, no goals, no badges, no cheerleading — just a plain record of your day, kept private on your phone.

Everything stays on your device

There's no account to create and no server behind this app. Your entries live in local storage on your iPhone, full stop. Nothing is uploaded, analyzed, or shared.

Leave whenever you want

Export everything as a CSV file in one tap — it opens straight in Excel, Numbers, or Google Sheets. An app that makes it easy to walk away is one that doesn't need to trap you.
