How do I get my data out?

Open Settings and tap Export as CSV. This creates a file with every entry you've logged and hands it to the iOS share sheet, so you can save it to Files, AirDrop it, email it to yourself, or open it directly in a spreadsheet app.

How do I delete an entry?

Swipe left on any entry, on the Today screen or in a Calendar day, to delete it. Tap an entry to edit its description or calorie count instead.

How do I erase everything?

Open Settings and tap Erase all data. You'll be asked to confirm, since this permanently deletes every entry on your device.

How do I switch between light and dark mode?

Open Settings → Appearance and choose System, Light, or Dark. System follows your phone's own setting automatically.
